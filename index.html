About Cedric's Automatic Pizzeria

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by robotics engineer Cedric, our pizzeria combines a passion for authentic Italian pizza with cutting-edge automation technology.

After years of perfecting both pizza recipes and robotic systems, Cedric created a revolutionary pizza-making process that guarantees consistency, quality, and speed without sacrificing the artisanal touch.

Every pizza is crafted with precision by our robotic chefs, overseen by our human pizza artisans who ensure each pie meets our exacting standards.

Our Technology

Our proprietary automation system handles everything from dough preparation to perfect ingredient distribution, ensuring each pizza is made with millimeter precision.

  • AI-powered dough stretching for the perfect crust
  • Computer vision sauce and cheese application
  • Precision topping placement algorithms
  • Smart oven temperature control

Our Ingredients

While our process is automated, our commitment to quality ingredients is entirely human.

  • Organic flour milled daily for our dough
  • Fresh mozzarella from local dairy farms
  • San Marzano tomatoes imported from Italy
  • Fresh herbs grown in our vertical farm
